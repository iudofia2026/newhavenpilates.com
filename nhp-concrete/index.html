Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to get started

Do I need experience to join a class?

No experience necessary! We offer introductory classes specifically designed for beginners. Our instructors provide modifications for all fitness levels.

What should I wear to class?

Wear comfortable workout clothes that allow for movement. We recommend form-fitting attire so instructors can check your alignment. Grip socks are required for equipment classes.

How do I book my first class?

Email Krista at krista@newhavenpilates.com to schedule your introductory session. We'll discuss your goals and find the perfect class for you.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows will be charged the full class rate.

Do you offer packages or memberships?

Yes! We offer various packages from 5-class packs to unlimited monthly memberships. All packages are valid for 6 months from purchase.
