ConcreteClub

With over 10 years of industry experience, I like to join forces with my artist friends to refine the border between digital and analog worlds.

Basically, instead of hiring an agency, you get to work with a self-organized team of friends who already spend most of their time together.

We’ve collaborated with brands and studios such as Antinomy, Ashfall, Chanel, Lufthansa, McCann New York, Microsoft, Tambien, Paris Saint-Germain, Pattern...

(1) The Concrete Club won't ever let creativity down. For any reason.

(2) The Concrete Club works with other people, not for them.

(3) At least two artists per collaboration.

(4) The Concrete Club artists share a common love for design, digital technologies and art in general.

(5) The Concrete Club is about thinking, designing, building and challenging.

(6) But More than all, the Concrete Club artists are dear friends.
